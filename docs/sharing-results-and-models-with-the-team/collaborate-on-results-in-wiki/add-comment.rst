Insert a comment
================

When you hover on any content in the Wiki page, the **Comment** icon appears on the right side. Click it to make a comment:

    .. image:: ../../_static/images/sharing-results-and-models-with-the-team/collaborate-on-results-in-wiki/new_wiki_comment.png
        :target: ../../_static/images/sharing-results-and-models-with-the-team/collaborate-on-results-in-wiki/new_wiki_comment.png
        :alt: New Wiki comment
        :width: 800


Alternatively, select existing text and click the comment button in the context menu that appears:


    .. image:: ../../_static/images/sharing-results-and-models-with-the-team/collaborate-on-results-in-wiki/new_wiki_comment_menu.png
